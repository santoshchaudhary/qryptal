Fraud in healthcare — Information manipulation is rampant and here is how to tackle it with an elegant solution.

Mon Nov 13, 2017 by Rahul Sinha

One of the areas where fraud is rampant and can have serious consequences is healthcare. Normally this manipulation of information is done for specific monetary gains — e.g inflating the bills for claiming a larger amount from insurance or medical reimbursement or even generating fictitious bills for procedures never carried out. There are also cases where doctors have been practicing impudently for years on the back of fake credentials and caused material damage to unsuspecting patients. Read more →
